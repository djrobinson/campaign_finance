Got It!
The Super PAC expenditures treemap represents all funds spent by a given Super PAC
The overall size of the rectangle represents all funds spent by the committee. Each smaller rectangle represents the portion of funds that were given to just one recipient.
Clicking each rectangle will show a transaction by transaction breakout of each expense. Each level of granularity is explained in the instructions below.
Hover over any square to see the preview in the toolbar above. This will show a preview of the amount, recipient, and the purpose of the transaction.
